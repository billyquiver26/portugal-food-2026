Before you go
  • Book the top picks. July is peak season — reserve the dark-card spots a day or two ahead, especially the small ones (DAMA, A Nossa Casa, Cabaças). Many seat 16–30 people.
  • Meal times run late. Lunch ~12:30–3, dinner ~7:30–10:30. Kitchens at some spots don't open until 7. A few close one or two days a week — closures are noted on each card.
  • Carry some cash. A handful of the best old-school grills (Churrasqueira da Paz in Lisbon, a couple of Porto taverns) are cash-only.
  • Healthy choices everywhere. Grilled meats, chicken, and big salads are easy to find everywhere. Each leg also has dedicated lighter/veg-forward spots marked 🌿.
